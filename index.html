The Library Website

Features:

  • Library Accounts: Libraries can add and manage their inventory of books, movies, comics, and more. Items that are checked out are updated with due dates and borrower information.
  • Borrower Accounts: Borrowers can track the items they have checked out, including due dates. They can also receive reminders for returns or renewals.
  • Helps Libraries: Provides a comprehensive view of inventory and helps librarians identify overdue items and contact borrowers.
  • Helps Borrowers: Works with items from any library and ensures borrowers never miss a due date.

This website is useful if you're:

  • A researcher
  • An avid reader
  • A library employee
  • A teachers borrowing textbooks for students
  • Anyone who has trouble remembering due dates
